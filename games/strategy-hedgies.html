About Strategy - Hedgies

About Hedgies: Lead your team of armed hedgehogs into battle in this turn-based artillery game. Similar to classics like Worms, you'll take turns aiming and firing a variety of wacky weapons at your opponents. Account for wind and terrain to land the perfect shot and be the last team standing!

Features classic turn-based artillery gameplay, a wide arsenal of fun and destructive weapons, customizable hedgehog teams, and multiplayer support.

How to Play Strategy - Hedgies

Movement: Use A/D or Arrow Keys to move your hedgehog.

Aiming: Use W/S or Arrow Keys to aim your weapon.

Shooting: Press Spacebar to fire.

Special: Use the Mouse to select weapons from your inventory.

Goal: How to Play Hedgies: Your goal is to eliminate the opposing hedgehog team using your arsenal of weapons. Take turns moving, aiming, and firing. The last team with hedgehogs remaining wins the match.
