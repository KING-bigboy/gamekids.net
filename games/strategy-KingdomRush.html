About Kingdom Rush

Kingdom Rush is an addictive tower defense game where you must defend your kingdom from hordes of enemies. Build towers, upgrade your defenses, and deploy heroes to protect your land from the invading forces.

The game features a variety of towers, each with its own unique abilities and strengths. You must strategically place your towers to maximize their effectiveness and defend against the diverse range of enemies. With its challenging gameplay and engaging storyline, Kingdom Rush offers hours of fun for tower defense enthusiasts.

How to Play Kingdom Rush

Build Towers: Click on the tower icons at the bottom of the screen to build towers. Each tower costs resources, so manage your resources carefully.

Upgrade Towers: Upgrade your towers to increase their power and effectiveness.

Deploy Heroes: Deploy heroes to the battlefield to provide additional support and defend against the enemy hordes.

Defend Your Kingdom: Protect your kingdom from the invading forces and prevent them from reaching your base.
