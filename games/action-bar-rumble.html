About Action-Bar Rumble

About Bar Rumble: Things have gotten out of hand at the local tavern. In this chaotic brawler, your goal is to throw punches, chairs, and anything else you can find to knock out everyone else and be the sole survivor of the rumble.

The game features chaotic, physics-based fighting, interactive environments with throwable objects, and hilarious multiplayer brawls.

How to Play Action-Bar Rumble

Movement: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move.

Aiming: N/A

Shooting: Use Left Mouse Button to punch. Use Right Mouse Button to pick up/throw objects.

Special: Press Spacebar to jump or dodge.

Goal: How to Play Bar Rumble: Knock out other players by punching them or hitting them with objects. Your goal is to be the last person standing in the bar.
