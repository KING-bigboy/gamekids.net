About Puzzle - Kingdom Solitaire

About Kingdom Solitaire: Enjoy the classic game of Klondike Solitaire with a charming medieval theme. Your goal is to sort all the cards into the four foundation piles, building each suit up from Ace to King.

The game features classic Klondike Solitaire rules, a relaxing medieval theme, and helpful hint and undo buttons.
