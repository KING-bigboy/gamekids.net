About Idle-Inventor

About Idle Inventor: A game of logistics, automation, and industrial might. Start with basic resources and manually craft simple components. As you progress, you'll unlock complex machinery to automate every step of the process. Design efficient production lines, research new technologies, and build an industrial empire that spans from wooden carts to powerful tanks.

Complex automation and crafting system. A massive tech tree to research. Satisfying logistical puzzles to solve. Build and manage intricate production chains.

How to Play Idle-Inventor

Movement: Pan the camera with WASD or by holding the Middle Mouse Button.

Aiming: N/A

Shooting: Left-click to place machines and select menu items. Right-click to remove machines.

Special: Use the Mouse Wheel to zoom in and out.

Goal: How to Play Idle Inventor: The goal is to build a fully automated factory. Start by placing basic resource harvesters. Connect them to crafters using conveyor belts to create more complex items. Sell these items for cash, which you can use to research new technologies and unlock more advanced machinery. Optimize your layout for maximum efficiency.
